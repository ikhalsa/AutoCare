Services

Diagnostic Services

Is your vehicle running bad or stalling? Is your check engine light on? The issue could be as simple as a loose gas cap or as complex as internal engine repair. Either way, Auto Care can help. Pairing up state of the art equipment with our professional certified technicians, gives us the ability to diagnose the concern and determine what repairs are needed to get you safely back on the road.

Repair Services

We understand car repair can be a stressful event. We strive to provide our customers with comprehensive information as to why the repair is required, how the component failure occurred, and what preventative maintenance measures can be taken to prolong the success of the repair and avoid failure in the future. Our professional ASE-certified technicians are trained to diagnose and repair broken or faulty system components that make up your vehicle’s complex systems, including the heating and cooling, brake, steering and suspension systems and more.
